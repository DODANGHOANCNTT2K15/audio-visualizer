# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want a small always-on desktop audio visualizer while listening to music or working on Windows.

## Product Purpose

AudioVisualizer shows the current system audio as a lightweight floating spectrum near the desktop edge. Success means the visualizer feels responsive, unobtrusive, and pleasant without getting in the user's way.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, fluid, musical.

## Anti-references

Avoid heavy overlays, distracting decorative motion, high-latency visual feedback, and window chrome that makes the visualizer feel like a full application.

## Design Principles

- Motion should feel tied to the audio, not like a separate animation layer.
- Keep the window lightweight and peripheral so it can live on top of other work.
- Prefer smooth transform-based changes over layout-heavy effects.
- Respect system preferences such as high contrast and Windows accent color.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use restrained motion, avoid flashing, preserve high-contrast compatibility, and keep the visualizer nonessential so users can ignore it without losing function.
